Walk-in high and low temperature (humid and hot) test chamber
The walk-in high and low temperature test chamber is used for adaptability testing of electrical and electronic products, their original components, and other materials stored, transported, and used in high and low temperature environments.
Learn more
Large three box cold and hot shock test chamber
The large three box cold and hot impact test box is an essential testing equipment in the metal, plastic, rubber, electronics and other material industries. Used to test the degree to which a material structure or composite material can withstand continuous environments of extreme high and low temperatures in an instant, and to detect chemical changes or physical damage caused by thermal expansion and contraction of the sample in the shortest possible time.

ABOUT US

HaiBoe

Dongguan Haibo Energy Environmental Testing Equipment Co., Ltd. was established in early 2016 and is located in Xinli Outsourcing Industrial Park, Chabu Town, Dongguan City, Guangdong Province. The core founder of the company has been working for Suzhou Testing and a large domestic joint venture reliability testing equipment company with strong technical research and development capabilities.
